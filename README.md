# RootSpace: User-Owned Knowledge Space

Stop scattering your knowledge across AI agents.

AI agents shouldn't each own a piece of your data. RootSpace flips this model. Maintain one knowledge space that you own, and let agents connect to it.

One Source. Your Control. Zero Fragmentation.
